Spicy Samosa
Mix  plain flour dough with a pinch of salt,baking powder and melted butter
knead it into a soft dough 
Mash boiled potatoes with pinch of turmeric powder,salt to taste, spices powder to taste and 3 tsb sunflower oil 
Roll the dough into circular shape and cut into half , in the half pastry fill the spicy mashed potato filling and join into cyllindrical samosa shapes!
deep fry in sunflowerr oil and your spicy samosas are ready !
